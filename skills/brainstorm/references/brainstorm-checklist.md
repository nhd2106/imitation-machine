# Brainstorm Checklist

Use this during brainstorming to avoid jumping from idea to plan too early.

## Required understanding

- What problem exists today?
- Who experiences it?
- What outcome counts as success?
- What is explicitly out of scope?
- What constraints already exist in the repo or product?

## Design readiness checks

- Could two engineers implement this the same way?
- Are acceptance criteria pass/fail rather than vibes?
- Is the request one coherent requirement, not multiple systems bundled together?
- Is the recommended approach justified against alternatives?

## Red flag language

Stop and clarify if the request still depends on words like:

- simple
- just
- obvious
- make it better
- improve this
- modernize it
